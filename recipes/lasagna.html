Lasagna

Description

A delicious, homemade lasagna recipe that anyone can make! Simple ingredients ensure a quick meal that tastes great and is easy to eat over and over again.

This savory dish makes use of a creamy ricotta cheese mixture and a beef and sausage meat sauce. Great for make-ahead and freezing or an easy weeknight meal with lots of leftovers!

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 lbs. ground beef
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

Prep

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl.

Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and the diced onion. Cook for 10 minutes
  2. Add ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high. Break up meat into small bits and cook for 8-10 minutes. Add garlic during last minutes
  3. Add chicken broth, marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil then cover and reduce to a simmer.

Cook Noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook until al dente. Gently stir while cooking.
  3. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. Lay the cooled noodles on parchment paper.

Assemble

  1. Spread a cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch casserole dish. Next, add 4 lasagna noodles to bottom.
  2. Spread 1/3 of cheese mixture over noodles followed by 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce
  3. Repeat 2 more times, adding two additional layers.
  4. Top with 2 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray. Place spray side down on top of dish.

Bake

  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 10 additional minutes.
  2. Remove from over and garnish with fresh parsley. Let stand for 15 minutes prior to serving to allow to set.
