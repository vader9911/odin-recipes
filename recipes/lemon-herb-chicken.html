Lemon Herb Chicken

Description

This lemon herb chicken is, simple, easy and delicious dish. With a handful of easy ingredients this tasty chicken goes great with almost any side.

Ingredients

  1. 2, 5oz, chicken skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  2. 1 medium lemon, juiced
  3. 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. 1 pinch oregano
  5. 1 tbsp parsley
  6. pepper and salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a bowlpour 1/2 lemon juice over the top and season with salt.
  2. Heat oil in medium pan, over medium-low heat. Add chicken to the hot oil, along with the remaining lemon juice and oregano. Season with salt pepper. Saut√© until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5 to 10 minutes per side.
  3. Remove from heat. Garnish with parsley and serve
