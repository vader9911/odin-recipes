Pesto Shrimp

Description

This creamy, easy pesto shrimp is as simple as it is delicious!

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb linguine pasta
  2. 1/2 cup butter
  3. 2 cups heavy cream
  4. 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  5. 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  6. 1/3 cup pesto
  7. 1 lb large shrimp, peeled

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil. Stir in linguine and return to a boil. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally until tender. About 8-10 minutes; drain.
  2. While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in cream and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, for 6-8 minutes.
  3. Stir parmesan into cream sauce until thick. Add pesto. cook until thickened, 3-5 minutes.
  4. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink; about 5 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over hot linguine.
